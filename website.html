Peru:A Dream Getaway

A fun place to go.

Over the summer I went to Lima, Peru and Cusco, Peru with a high elevation of 11,00 feet from sea level The cities were beautiful and below I will show you pictures of my journey


In Peru, they showed us their culture, popular monuments and resturaunts in their city. We first traveled to Lima and viewed popular shopping areas and the lovely park. At the lovely park one ca see the "el beso" which can be translated at the kiss. A kissing statue marks the middle of the park. My favorite resturants were called "Popular"and "Mangos." After a few days in Lima, we took a plane to Urumbamba, Peru and stayed in the Sacred Valley at the Casa Andina hotel. As the days went by, my family and I visited famous churches and viewed different festivals. We took a tour with "Condor travels" and saw the Sacsayhuaman landmark. After the tour, we took a first class train ride to Macchu Picchu and hiked up difficult trails to see macchu picchu in different angles.
